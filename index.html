Services & Products

RB1 Coal (Premium Grade)

High-quality thermal coal with a calorific value of around 6,000 kcal/kg (adb), ideal for power generation and industrial applications.

RB2 Coal (Standard Industrial Grade)

Mid-tier grade with a calorific value of approximately 5,500 kcal/kg (adb), widely used in power plants and industrial furnaces.

RB3 Coal (Economical Utility Grade)

Economical option with a calorific value of around 4,800 kcal/kg (adb), suitable for power stations prioritizing affordability.

Run-of-Mine (ROM) Coal

Unprocessed coal directly extracted from mines, ideal for clients with specific processing facilities.
