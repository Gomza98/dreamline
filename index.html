About Us

Dreamline Corporation is a leading coal and chrome trader, offering high-quality, customized solutions for domestic and international clients. With a strong emphasis on quality, efficiency, and customer satisfaction, the company supplies a range of products—including RB1, RB3, and ROM coal—to industries like power generation, steel manufacturing, and industrial processing. Dreamline stands out for its deep industry expertise, reliable delivery, and strict quality control. Committed to sustainable, long-term partnerships, the company leverages advanced technology and best practices to remain at the forefront of the sector, making it a trusted partner for businesses of all sizes.

Services & Products

RB1 Coal (Premium Grade)

High-quality thermal coal with a calorific value of around 6,000 kcal/kg (adb), ideal for power generation and industrial applications.

RB2 Coal (Standard Industrial Grade)

Mid-tier grade with a calorific value of approximately 5,500 kcal/kg (adb), widely used in power plants and industrial furnaces.

RB3 Coal (Economical Utility Grade)

Economical option with a calorific value of around 4,800 kcal/kg (adb), suitable for power stations prioritizing affordability.

Run-of-Mine (ROM) Coal

Unprocessed coal directly extracted from mines, ideal for clients with specific processing facilities.
